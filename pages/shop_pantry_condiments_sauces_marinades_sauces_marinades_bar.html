				If You Do Not See Your Store Listed:			




1. Hover over the “My Store” menu item.

2. Click on “Store Locator”

3. Scroll down to find the location titled “Your Commissary”.

4. Click “Make This My Store”.

This will be your default location to view and clip coupons until your local commissary is added to the Commissary CLICK2GO website.














				Locating Your Rewards Card Number			




Look on the back of your Rewards Card and enter the “4” and the next 10 digits. You should ignore the very last number.
